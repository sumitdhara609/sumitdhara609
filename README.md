#### Execution Intelligence Platform

ChronoForge is a future-simulation and execution-intelligence platform designed to help ambitious individuals evaluate whether their goals can realistically survive time, effort, capacity, uncertainty, and pressure.

It studies the relationship between ambition and execution through structured computational logic — transforming subjective planning into measurable projections, feasibility signals, and risk-aware recommendations.
